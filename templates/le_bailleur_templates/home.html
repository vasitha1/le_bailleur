About LeBailleur

Our Inspiration

LeBailleur was born from a deeply personal challenge faced by my 79-year-old father, a dedicated landlord managing a portfolio of 80 tenants. Every month, he would struggle with a complex system of receipts, attempting to track who owes what ‚Äì be it rent, electricity, light, or wifi.

This journey began with a simple observation: managing rental properties can be overwhelmingly complicated, especially for seasoned landlords dealing with multiple tenants and various utility payments.

Although this is just the Minimum Viable Product (MVP), we believe LeBailleur will provide a transformative solution to simplify property management and bring clarity to landlords' financial tracking.
